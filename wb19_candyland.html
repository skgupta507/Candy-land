THINGS TO DO

Fun and imaginative things to do in Candyland

Chocolate Factory Tour

Go on a guided tour of the magical Chocolate Factory, where you can witness the chocolate waterfall and even create your custom chocolate bar.

Candy Carnival

Enjoy the thrill of candy-themed carnival rides and games, from the Taffy Tornado to the Gummy Bear Ferris Wheel.

Lollipop Forest Hike

Take a stroll through the enchanted Lollipop Forest, where colossal lollipops of every flavor grow on edible trees.

Candy Art Gallery

Visit a candy-themed art gallery featuring sculptures, paintings, and installations made entirely of candy.

Candy River Boat Ride

Embark on a candy river boat ride down the Gumdrop River, where you can dip your hand in for a taste of the sweet flowing water.

Cotton Candy Cloud Picnic

Have a whimsical picnic on cotton candy clouds with an assortment of cotton candy flavors and other sugary treats.

Flavors

Explore the wide variety of candy types, from chocolates and gummies to hard candies and licorice.
